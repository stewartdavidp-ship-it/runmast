AI Transparency

Mast Platform · Last Updated March 29, 2026

Why This Page Exists

Mast is an AI-powered platform. Unlike many companies that bury AI usage in fine print, we believe you deserve a clear, honest explanation of how AI works in our platform — what data it touches, what it doesn't, and how you stay in control.

How Mast Uses AI

Mast uses AI to help Tenant Owners (merchants) run their businesses more effectively. AI powers the following features:

Social Media Content Generation

  • What it does: Generates suggested social media posts, product descriptions, and captions based on your product catalog.
  • What data is used: Product names, descriptions, prices, images, and your business context (business name, style, target audience).
  • What you control: You review, edit, and approve all generated content before it's used anywhere. Nothing is published automatically.

Studio Assistant

  • What it does: A conversational AI assistant that can answer questions about your business, help with product descriptions, brainstorm ideas, and provide guidance.
  • What data is used: Your prompts (what you type/ask), plus relevant business context (product catalog, business profile) to provide informed answers.
  • What you control: You initiate every conversation. The assistant only responds to what you ask.

Image Classification

  • What it does: Analyzes product photos to identify and categorize items, helping organize your catalog.
  • What data is used: Product images (sent as image data to the AI for visual analysis) and any training images you've provided for your product categories.
  • What you control: You choose which images to classify and confirm or correct the results.

Show and Event Research

  • What it does: Researches craft shows, markets, and events that may be relevant to your business.
  • What data is used: Your business context (product type, location, business description) to find relevant events.
  • What you control: You initiate each research request and decide which results to act on.

Booth Detection (Events Module)

  • What it does: Analyzes floor plan images to detect booth numbers and grid positions for event management.
  • What data is used: Floor plan images uploaded by event organizers.
  • What you control: Event organizers upload images and review detected booth layouts.

What Data Goes to AI

When you use an AI feature, the following types of data may be sent to our AI provider:

Included:

  • Product names, descriptions, and prices
  • Product images
  • Business name and profile information
  • Your prompts and questions
  • Business context relevant to the specific feature

Never included:

  • Shopper payment information (credit cards, bank accounts)
  • Shopper email addresses or mailing addresses
  • Shopper order details or purchase history
  • Account passwords or authentication credentials
  • Other Tenant Owners' data

Who Provides the AI

Our AI provider is Anthropic, the maker of Claude. Anthropic is a US-based AI safety company.

  • Anthropic's Privacy Policy: anthropic.com/privacy
  • Anthropic's Usage Policy: anthropic.com/usage-policy

Does Anthropic Train on Your Data?

Under Anthropic's current API terms (as of March 2026), data sent through the API is not used to train their models. This means:

  • Your product data is not used to improve Anthropic's AI.
  • Your prompts and conversations are not added to training datasets.
  • Your images are not retained for model development.
  • Your business context does not influence how the AI works for anyone else.

Mast uses Anthropic's commercial API and does not opt in to any programs that would allow your data to be used for training. If Anthropic's API terms change in a way that affects these commitments, we will update this page and notify Tenant Owners via email.

Data sent to Anthropic is processed for your request and then handled according to Anthropic's data retention policies for API usage.

How AI Processing Works

AI requests in Mast follow this path:

  1. You trigger an AI feature in the Mast admin app (e.g., click "Generate Social Post").
  2. Mast's server prepares the request — assembling relevant product data and context.
  3. Mast's Cloud Function sends the request to Anthropic's API. The request goes from Mast's server to Anthropic's server. Your browser never communicates directly with Anthropic.
  4. Anthropic processes the request and returns a response.
  5. Mast's server returns the result to your browser for your review.

At no point does your browser send data directly to Anthropic. All AI communication is server-to-server.

Prompt and Conversation Logging

When you use AI features, Mast does not store your prompts or AI responses in the database. AI interactions are processed in memory by Cloud Functions and the results are returned to your browser. Token usage (cost) is logged for billing purposes, but the content of your prompts and AI responses is not retained by Mast after the request completes.

Note: Google Cloud Functions may capture limited request metadata in platform logs (Cloud Logging) as part of standard infrastructure operation. These logs are subject to Google Cloud's retention policies and are not accessible to other tenants or used by Mast for any purpose beyond infrastructure monitoring.

How AI Usage Is Metered

Mast uses a token-based system to track and limit AI usage:

  • Each subscription tier includes a monthly allocation of coins (1 coin = 100 AI tokens).
  • Each AI request consumes tokens based on the complexity of the input and output.
  • Your current balance and usage are visible in your admin dashboard.
  • When your balance reaches zero, AI features are paused until your next monthly reset or a plan upgrade.

This system ensures transparent, predictable AI costs. There are no surprise charges — you control your AI usage within your plan's limits.

AI Content Disclaimer

AI-generated content is a tool, not a final product. You should:

  • Review all AI-generated text before publishing to social media, your storefront, or anywhere else.
  • Verify factual claims in AI-generated research or descriptions.
  • Edit for your voice — AI suggestions are a starting point, not a replacement for your creative judgment.
  • Check for accuracy — AI can occasionally produce incorrect or outdated information.

You are responsible for any content you publish, whether or not it was generated with AI assistance.

Your Control

AI in Mast is designed around a simple principle: you're in charge.

  • Opt-in per use. Every AI feature requires you to actively invoke it. There is no background AI processing of your data.
  • No automatic publishing. AI generates suggestions. You decide what gets published.
  • Visible usage. Your token balance shows exactly how much AI you're using.
  • Your own API key. If you prefer, you can configure your own Anthropic API key, giving you a direct relationship with Anthropic and full visibility into your API usage through Anthropic's dashboard.
